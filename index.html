Admissions

We welcome new students from all backgrounds.
How to Apply:

  1. Pick up an application form at the school office or download from our website.
  2. Submit completed forms with required documents.
  3. Attend an interview/assessment (if required).
  4. Successful applicants will be notified by phone or email.
